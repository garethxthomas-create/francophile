Official syllabus

Goals adapted from the CEFR standard behind the official DELF and DALF exams (France Education International), levels A1 to C2. The placement test finds your starting point per skill; checkpoint tests then verify goals one by one. Oral skills are practiced in Chat but cannot be formally verified by typing.
